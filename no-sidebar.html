About Me

It's been a heck of a journey.

Born and raised in Charlottesville, Virginia, I am a Software Engineer living in the San Francisco Bay Area. I love learning languages, not only programming and scripting languages but also natural languages, such as Japanese, Portuguese, and Russian. My life was changed by the desktop application Mnemosyne, and it was from this experience that I found my passion for programming. One of the happiest moments of my life was completing my own Web application based off the Mnemosyne application. I am a basketball fanatic, stand-up comedian, and like to do random things like running up Nob Hill.

I have an undergraduate BA in Mathematics-Economics from Reed College, a liberal arts college located in Portland, Oregon, where I graduated in 2014. After graduating and working for a year, I moved to San Francisco and completed App Academy, a 3-month software development program with under a 5% acceptance rate. The curriculum covered full-stack web development, algorithms, and coding best practices and patterns. My favorite frameworks are Backbone.JS and Famo.us, and nothing pleases me more than a difficult Ruby problem or improving performance on ugly ActiveRecord and SQL queries.
